About Me

My design journey began a long time ago I suppose.

I've always liked solving problems, and certainly dislike the ones that remain unsolved. Sketching, interacting with computers, and the ideation opportunities they provide has certainly been and remains interesting to me.

After graduating with a degree in Graphic Design, I quickly learned that design had a much larger scope than I'd anticipated. Front-end coding was the primary addition to my skill set, but I love jumping into new software and dabbling in new skills when the opportunity arises.

Currently, I love bringing Design Thinking into most everything I work on. The ability to step back and really think about the problems we're trying to solve and make sure we're on the right path is incredibly valuable.

As for hobbies, and outside of experimenting in design, I'm always looking forward to my next adventure in video games, books, and film.

Here's looking forward to the journey that remains.

Skillset: Project/Client Consultation, Brand Identity, User Experience Design, Information Architecture, Responsive Website Design, Custom Wordpress Development, Strive for Accessibility, Cross Browser Support/Device Testing, Prototypes, Confident, Open to Critical Feedback
