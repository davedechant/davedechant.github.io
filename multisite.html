GERTY Design System CPT Multisite Themes Plus Driveby Companion App

CPT Multisite

Tools: Balsamic, Sketch, Invision, WordPress, Adobe Creative Suite, Sublime Text

Problem

ConversionPoint Technologies is made up of several subsidiaries. The websites for these entities and their products were inconsistent and updating them meant developer resources or logging into various Content Management Systems.

Solution

The solutions came down to unifying not only the experience across the various websites, but also making it easier for people to access and make edits to the websites they were responsible for.

Outcome

Having experience creating WordPress multisites and with the help of my team, the CPT multisite was created. It takes advantage of a core theme to make experiences consistent, and users can login to a unified framework to edit any site.
