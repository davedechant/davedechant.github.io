Driveby Companion App

Tools: Sketch, Invision

Problem

Driveby is a product meant for larger screens because of the information and interactions involved. I wanted to look into options that would allow for smaller, but meaningful interactions on mobile devices.

Solution

I spoke with some of our core users about how being able to quickly check and possibly update specific, yet limited aspects within the product on a mobile device could be beneficial.

Outcome

The outcome was this current prototype of a Driveby companion app that would allow the user to quickly access and update information when on the go or in a situation where a larger screen wasn't an option.
