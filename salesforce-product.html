Salesforce Service Console

Tools: Miro, Balsamic, Lightning Design System, Sketch, Invision, Adobe Creative Suite

Problem

Securian customer service agents (CSAs) used myriad disparate systems to help customers. Besides difficulty cross-training between business units (BU), CSAs service time was ineffecient from the need to toggle between these systems.

Solution

We worked directly with the CSAs and BU managers to see how they used current systems, performed system audits, and created current state service blueprints and userflows. This user research was used to create future state ecosystem flows and prototypes, with CSAs and key stakeholders involved throughout the entire process.

Outcome

Final deliverables and an initial three week sprint plan were presented to the Securian Agile team, BU managers, and project managers. Based on these deliverables and discovery findings, a scope of work for the build process was approved that same day.
