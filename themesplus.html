Themes Plus

Tools: WordPress, Adobe Creative Suite, Sublime Text

Problem

More and more at ThinkSEM we came across small to medium business clients that needed quality marketing websites, but didn't necessarily have the budget for our custom website services.

Solution

We needed the ability to significantly cut our development time to offer a lower cost solution to these clients. In my spare time, I had been learning about theme development, and began thinking of ways to utilize them.

Outcome

I created an initial set of themes that took into consideration the most common needs of our clients. This turned into Themes Plus, a library of themes that our clients could choose from at a much lower cost.
