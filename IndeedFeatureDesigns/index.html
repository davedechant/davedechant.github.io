Problem Statement

Today, when a job seeker needs to edit a submitted application, they cannot.

This is unacceptable, because the job seeker may need to edit an important piece of information vital to qualifying for the job.

We envision a world where any job seeker can easily and quickly edit any application submitted on Indeed's platform. We are bringing this world about by creating an Edit My Application feature that is easy to find and use, and gives job seekers the confidence that their application is always up to date.
